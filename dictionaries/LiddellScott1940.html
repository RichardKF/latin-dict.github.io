A Greek-English Lexicon, Liddell & Scott (1940)

Long standing and popular LSJ dictionary of Ancient Greek language, transcribed by Perseus project and reproduced on many web-sites.

Download

Current version is v1.2 (February 14, 2020). Format Slob is recommended for GoldenDict, read documentation about other dictionary shells.

Another variant of this dictionary, in StarDict format, can be taken from the Chaeréphon’s collection.

States and limitations

Headwords are represented in tree forms:

  • Greek script polytonic: φιλέω
  • Greek script w/o diacritics: φιλεω
  • Latin transcription: filew
