Ancient Greek lemmatiser

Ancient Greek lemmatiser is a lemmatiser for Ancient Greek texts. Our adaptaion contains lemmatization, morphological analyzing, and short English definitions (one-two words).

This dictionary was superseded with off-line Morphologia Graeca and will not be updated in the future.

Download

Download the most recent version and add file index.html to GoldenDict following the guide for HTML dictionaries.

Exemplum

States and limitations

Comparing to Alpheios, this dictionary is tolerant to incorrect or absent tones and breathing marks, but someitmes fails to recognize some declension or conjugation forms. Also, Greek words can be typed in transliteration:

αβγδεζηθικλμνξοπρςστυφχψωϝϟ
abgdezhqiklmncoprsstufxywfk
