Whitaker’s Words

Whitaker’s Words is a morphological analyser and simple Latin-English dictionary made by William Whitaker in 1993–2007. It contains about 39,000 articles and can do several trick to recognize words with variate spelling or Medieval forms.

Download

Download the most recent version and add file index.html to GoldenDict following the guide for HTML dictionaries.
