Cassell’s Latin Dictionary, Simpson (1959)

Latin–English college-level dictionary. Explanations are clear, not overloaded with excessive citations or rare meanings. Digital edition contains only Latin-English part of the book, and was supplemented with undefined number of articles from other sources.

Download

Current version is v1.0 (August 23, 2019). Format Slob is recommended for GoldenDict, read documentation about other dictionary shells.

States and limitations

Basing on the information I know about the source book, it was published before 1963, and its copyright was not renewed (checked in Copyright Renewals database). Therefore, now the book is in the Public Domain due to copyright expiration.
