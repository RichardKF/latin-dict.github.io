Differentias, Richter (1750)

Full title is “Differentias quae in Ausonii Popmae De differentiis verborum libris amissae sunt”.

Adam Daniel Richter (1709-1782) was an editor of the Popma’s dictionary printed in 1741. In 1750, he published an addition with list of 62 groups of Latin “synonyms” (in wide sense of the word).
